Data Deletion Request

We respect your right to have your personal data deleted from our systems. This page explains how to request deletion of your data from the Market Vendor Show Finder app.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

When you request data deletion, we will permanently remove:

  • Your account information (email, profile data)
  • Your saved preferences and settings
  • Your location history within the app
  • Any other personal information associated with your account
⚠️ Important: Data deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Once your data is deleted, you will not be able to recover your account or any information associated with it.

How to Request Data Deletion

Option 1: Delete Within the App

You can delete your account directly from the app:

  1. Open the Market Vendor Show Finder app
  2. Go to Settings or Profile
  3. Look for "Delete Account" or "Delete My Data"
  4. Follow the prompts to confirm deletion

Option 2: Email Us

If you prefer, you can request deletion by contacting us directly:

Send an email to: drewnoles71@gmail.com

Include in your email:

  • Subject line: "Data Deletion Request"
  • The email address associated with your account
  • Confirmation that you want all your data deleted

Timeline

We will process your deletion request within 30 days of receiving it. You will receive a confirmation email once your data has been deleted.

What Happens After Deletion?

  • You will no longer be able to log into your account
  • All your personal data will be permanently removed from our systems
  • Some anonymized data may be retained for legal or analytical purposes, but it will not be linked to you

Questions?

If you have questions about data deletion or our privacy practices, please contact us at drewnoles71@gmail.com
